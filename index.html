Naval, Biliran, Philippines

Naval, officially the Municipality of Naval, is a 2nd class municipality and capital of the province of Biliran, Philippines. As an economic booming town, it is the center of trade and commerce of the entire island province, as well as to the neighboring proximate municipalities that are under the jurisdiction of the Leyte province.
