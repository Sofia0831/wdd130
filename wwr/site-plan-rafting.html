Overview

Purpose

The primary purpose of the Rafting website is to provide comprehensive information about rafting trips, promote services, and facilitate the planning and bookings of rafting adventures for enthusiasts of all levels, through a pleasant browsing experience.

Audience

Our target audience includes both individuals and groups spanning various ages and financial backgrounds interested in seeking thrilling yet safe outdoor adventures and expect detailed trip information and a user-friendly site, including water sport enthusiasts, tourists seeking fun experiences, and families desiring a safe and enjoyable rafting adventure interested in family-friendly packages and amenities. We provide an inclusive space for rafting enthusiasts of all levels.

Rafting and whitewater rafting are outdoor entertainment activities that use a boat or an unsinkable raft. It has different difficulty levels according to the current of the water in which the sport is done. It can be done solo but is more commonly done with groups and needs good teamwork to manage the water. River rafting, canoeing and whitewater rafting are the most preferred styles of this sport.

Whitewater rafting has more action than the traditional rafting. Conquering the river with teamwork and beating the fear of dangerous white water makes this sport very exciting. The best part of whitewater rafting which separates it from the other action sports is the chance to share the joy of the action with the beloved ones. It is 200 years old activity and shares a common history with rafting.
