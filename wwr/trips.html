Biliran Province is located in the Eastern Visayas region of the Philippines. Known for its stunning natural beauty, Biliran offers lush landscapes, serene rivers, and rich local culture. The Rapid Rush whitewater rafting experience takes place on the Biliran River, which flows through this picturesque province.


Location:

  • Biliran River
  • Culaba, Biliran, Biliran Province, Philippines
  • Town: Culaba Biliran

What to Expect:

  • Class II to IV rapids, offering a mix of thrilling challenges and manageable sections for beginners.
  • Verdant forests, scenic mountains, and the tranquil flow of the Biliran River.
  • Possible sightings of local bird species and other riverine wildlife.

Dates Available: Apr 3 - Apr 30

Cascade Canyon offers a thrilling whitewater rafting experience on the Biliran River. This adventure combines challenging rapids with the beauty of the river’s canyon scenery, providing an exciting experience for both beginners and experienced rafters.

What to Expect:
